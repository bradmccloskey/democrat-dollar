DemocratDollar Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Overview

DemocratDollar is a reference app that displays publicly available political donation data from the Federal Election Commission (FEC). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. DemocratDollar does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use.

Analytics

The app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage, browsing behavior, or any other activity within the app.

Data Displayed

All company donation data shown in the app comes from publicly available FEC records. This data is factual and sourced from official federal filings. No proprietary or private data is displayed.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Firebase Firestore to store and deliver company donation data. Firebase may collect basic technical information (such as device type and OS version) as part of its standard operation. Please refer to Google's Firebase Privacy Policy for details.

Data Storage

Company data is cached locally on your device for offline access. No personal data is stored on your device or our servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at democratdollar@proton.me.
